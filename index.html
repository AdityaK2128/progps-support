Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ProGPS: HUD Navigation ("the App") is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

The App accesses the following on-device sensors:

  • Location (GPS) — to display real-time speed, heading, altitude, and coordinates. Used only while the app is in use.
  • Motion & Barometer — to power the Motion Lab (G-force, attitude) and pressure altitude readings.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The App contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Data Storage

Waypoints and trip recordings you save are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded anywhere. You can delete them at any time from within the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
